Our idea :

Our new revolutionary machine will allow you to find your shoes brushed every morning before leaving for work. When you arrive home in the evening, you put your shoes in the machine and it's done! All you have to do is wait until the morning and it's done!

How to choose?

Between our different models there will be different options. The first one will be only for sneakers with a little complex dry cleaning. The second will allow you to make leather, suede and have beautiful shoes. Finally, there will be an all inclusive machine that will allow you to do everything at once.

Because you are tired of having dirty shoes, of getting them dirty at the first opportunity? Use our machines to have peace of mind. All you need is a night's sleep to get your shoes clean again.
